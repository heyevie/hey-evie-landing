Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We do not sell your data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

Hey Evie is an AI-powered content coaching platform built for mom creators. The app is operated by Hey Evie, accessible at tryheyevie.com and app.tryheyevie.com.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at heyevie@tryheyevie.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Your name and email address when you create an account or purchase a plan
  • Your profile information including your niche, content stage, and goals
  • Messages and content you share with Evie in the AI chat
  • Posts and comments you submit in the Mama Circle community
  • Notes and workshop responses you save within the app

Information collected automatically

  • Basic usage data such as which features you use and how often
  • Device type and browser information
  • Access codes and plan tier associated with your account

Payment information

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize your Hey Evie experience
  • To power Evie's AI responses with context about your niche and goals
  • To process your subscription or one-time purchase
  • To send you important account information and updates
  • To improve the app and develop new features
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not use your personal information for advertising purposes or sell it to third parties.

4. AI Chat and Data

Evie's AI responses are powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Messages you send to Evie are processed through this API to generate responses. We send your niche, name, and profile context along with your message to provide personalized coaching.

Your conversation history is stored locally on your device. We do not permanently store your full chat history on our servers beyond what is needed to process each individual request.

Please do not share sensitive personal information such as financial account numbers, social security numbers, or passwords in the chat.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Hey Evie uses your browser's local storage to save your profile, preferences, content plans, and progress so your experience persists between sessions. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

6. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only with the following third parties where necessary to operate the service:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Anthropic — AI response generation via the Claude API
  • Vercel — app hosting and deployment

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any other third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can view and update your profile information at any time in the ME tab of the app
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and personal data by emailing heyevie@tryheyevie.com
  • Data export: Contact us to request a copy of your data
  • Opt-out: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in the email footer

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your connection to Hey Evie is encrypted via HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Hey Evie is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at heyevie@tryheyevie.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you by email or through a notice in the app.
